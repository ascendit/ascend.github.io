Welcome to Ascend

Ascend is a platform for professional and career growth.

For Professionals

Are you looking to return to the workforce after a break? Are you actively seeking to grow in your current job by applying new skills?

Ascend matches your profile to projects at non-profits specifically targeting the skills you are looking to apply and enhance. It helps you collaborate with your fellow project mates, get endorsed for your work, and benchmark the skills you have gained. With 1-click posting, you can highlight your new skills and endorsements on leading job boards and networks like LinkedIn, and dramatically increase your chances of landing your dream job.

For Non-Profits

Ascend helps non-profits better partner with Corporations and Volunteers. It helps you recruit both corporate and private volunteers through its advanced matching algorithms.

Ascend's corporate volunteer management features help you better manage project complexity, ensure training and compliance with corporate social responsibility programs, increase volunteer engagement, and importantly add to your fundraising capacity. All in all, ultimately helping your organization achieve its mission.

For Companies

Today's workforce takes pride in working at a company that actively seeks to make a difference in its community. Ascend is a Corporate Social Responsibility platform that helps your employees discover skill-based volunteer opportunities at leading local and global non-profits. Through its matching and collaboration platform, Ascend can help your employees contribute and importantly measure the impact of their work.

Top professionals seek to work in companies that continuously create opportunities to learn and apply new skills. By focusing on high impact non-profits, Ascend creates opportunities for your employees to apply their skills and grow as professionals, creating an engaging work environment that attracts and retains top talent.
