Welcome to Ascend

Ascend is platform for personal and professional growth. Our platform helps people apply their skills to make a difference at non-profits, either directly, or through their employer.

For Job Candidates and Aspiring Professionals

Have you been away from the workforce and trying to exercise your skills in a safe learning environment? Are you actively seeking to grow in your current job by applying new skills?

Ascend matches your profile to projects at non-profits specifically targeting the skills you are looking to enhance. Upon joining a project, Ascend tracks your project engagement, endorsements from project-mates, as well as scores the skills you have gained. Ascend is integrated with multiple HR and Job platforms, making it easy to demonstrate your new Skills and expertise on Job boards and LinkedIn.

For Non-Profits

We help non-profits in their 3 biggest areas of focus:

  • Skill-based Volunteer recruitment
  • Corporate Volunteer management and fundraising
  • Measuring Volunteer engagement

For Companies

Ascend helps organizations increase employee engagement. Today's workfoce want their company to be committed to making a difference in the communities it operates in, and at the same time provide opportunties for growth and achievement.

Ascend is a Corporate Social Responsibility platform that helps match your employees to skill-based volunteer opportunities in areas they are passionate about. By focusing on skills, Ascend creates a safe learning and operating environment for your employees to practice new skills.
