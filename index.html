Welcome to Ascend

Ascend is a platform for professional and personal growth. We want to help users discover and work on skill-building projects, and get rewarded for their work.

For Users

Have you been away from the workforce and finding it difficult to get back in? By matching you to short-term skill building projects, Ascend can help you acquire, apply and importantly get endorsed for your skills. Ascend is connected to job networks like LinkedIn, and its easy to highlight the projects and your Ascend endorsements in your profile, helping you get discovered by prospective employers.

Are you actively seeking to grow in your current role, or move to a new role by acquiring new skills? Ascend can match you to opportunties for you to demonstrate your functional expertise and leadership in your professional.

Are you new to the workforce? Are you trying to discovering their own talents and aspirations? Ascend can help. With multiple projects.

At Ascend we are accelerating learning through application of your talent on skill-building projects at leading non-profit organizations. Grow professionally and personally by signing up on Ascend today.
