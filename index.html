For Job Candidates and Aspiring Professionals

Have you been away from the workforce and finding it difficult to get back in? Are you actively seeking to grow in your current role, or move to a new role by acquiring new skills? or are you joining the workforce and discovering talent and passion?

Leverage the Ascend platform for professional and personal growth. Apply your talent on skill-building projects at leading non-profit organizations, get endorsed for your work, and get discovered by recruiters and staffing companies.
