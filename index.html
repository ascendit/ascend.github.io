Welcome to Ascend

Ascend is platform for personal and professional growth. Our platform helps people apply their skills to make a difference at non-profits, either directly, or through their employer.

For Job Candidates and Aspiring Professionals

Have you been away from the workforce and finding it hard to return? Are you actively seeking to grow in your current job by applying new skills?

Ascend matches your profile to projects at non-profits specifically targeting the skills you are looking to enhance. Ascend tracks your project engagement, endorsements from project-mates, as well as scores the skills you have gained. With 1-click posting, you can demonstrate your new skills and expertise on Job boards and LinkedIn and land your dream role.

For Non-Profits

We help non-profits in their 3 biggest areas of focus:

  • Skill-based Volunteer recruitment
  • Corporate Volunteer management and fundraising
  • Measuring Volunteer engagement

For Companies

Today's workforce seek employers that actively engage and want to make a difference in the community they operate in. Employees are also looking for companies that create learning opportunties and apply new skills.

Ascend is a Corporate Social Responsibility platform that matches your employees to skill-based volunteer opportunities in local and global non-profits in areas they are passionate about. By focusing on skills, Ascend creates a safe learning and operating environment for your employees to apply their skills. With Ascend you can now measure the impact your company's employees are having on your community and create an engaging work environment that attracts and retains top talent.
