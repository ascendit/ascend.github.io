Welcome to Ascend

Ascend is a platform for personal and professional growth. It helps Professionals discover, collaborate and apply their skills to high-impact non-profit projects, either directly, or through their employer.

For Professionals

Are you looking to return to the workforce after a break? Are you actively seeking to grow in your current job by applying new skills?

Ascend matches your profile to projects at non-profits specifically targeting the skills you are looking to apply and enhance. It helps you collaborate with your fellow project mates, get endorsed for your work, and benchmark the skills you have gained. With 1-click posting, you can highlight your new skills and endorsements on leading job boards and networks like LinkedIn, and dramatically increase your chances of landing your dream job.

For Non-Profits

Ascend offers solutions to multiple complex problems that non-profits face. It helps non-profits recruit both corporate and private volunteers for key projects. It offers corporate volunteer management thereby decreasing project complexity, ensuring compliance to corporate social responsibility programs, and importantly improving your fundraising capacity. It also helps you measure volunteer engagement ultimately helping your organization achieve its mission.

For Companies

Today's workforce take pride in working with employers that actively seek to make a difference in the community they operate in. Professional seek work in companies that continuously create opportunities to learn and apply new skills.

Ascend is a Corporate Social Responsibility platform that helps your employees discover skill-based volunteer opportunities at leading local and global non-profits. By focusing on high impact non-profits, Ascend creates opportunities for your employees to apply their skills and grow as professionals. With Ascend you can now measure the impact your company's employees have on your community, and simultaneously create an engaging work environment that attracts and retains top talent.
